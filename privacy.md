# Privacy Policy for DevKits

**Last Updated:** March 30, 2026

## 1. Introduction

DevKits ("we", "our", or "us") operates [https://aiforeverthing.com](https://aiforeverthing.com) (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 No Data Storage
**All data processing happens client-side in your browser.** We do not store, transmit, or log any of the data you process through our tools.

### 2.2 Analytics
We use the following third-party analytics services:
- **Google Analytics** - Collects anonymous usage statistics
- **Microsoft Clarity** - Records anonymous session replays for UX improvement

These services may collect:
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Click patterns (anonymized)

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected data solely for:
- Understanding how users interact with our tools
- Improving user experience
- Identifying and fixing bugs
- Aggregated usage statistics

## 4. Data Sharing

We **do not**:
- Sell your personal data
- Share data with third parties (except analytics providers)
- Use your data for advertising targeting

## 5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:
- **Session cookies** - For basic functionality
- **Analytics cookies** - Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

## 6. Third-Party Services

Our Service links to the following third-party services:
- **Google Analytics** - [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Microsoft Clarity** - [Privacy Policy](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement)

## 7. Data Retention

- **Processed data**: Not stored (client-side only)
- **Analytics data**: Retained per third-party policies (Google Analytics: 26 months, Clarity: 30 days)

## 8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- HTTPS encryption for all connections
- No server-side storage of user data
- Regular security audits

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
